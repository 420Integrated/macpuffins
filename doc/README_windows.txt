Intro
-----
MacPuffins is a free open source project derived from Bitcoin, with
the goal of providing a long-term energy-efficient scrypt-based crypto-currency.
Built on the foundation of Bitcoin, PPCoin, NovaCoin, and Nebl - innovations such 
as proof-of-stake, onchain subcurrency tokens and sCrypt algorithms help further 
advance the field of crypto-currency.

MacPuffins is a Community Currency project with an imphasis on rebuilding and 
strengthening the small market economy and community interaction of Cape Breton 
Island, Nova Scotia.
